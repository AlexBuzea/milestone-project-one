Classes

Fitness classes are one of the most popular ways to stay fit, healthy and to improve your wellbeing. With a huge variety of classes available to participate in, Spartan Gym is the ideal place to get involved in a group exercise class.

Cardio Classes

Watch the video to take a look at the amazing range of bespoke group exercise classes we've developed to enhance your fitness experience. You're spoilt for choice, with a class to suit everybody.

Yoga Classes

One of the most ancient forms of exercise, Yoga is a gentle practice employing precision exercises, breathing & relaxation as a means of enhancing the union of the mind & body. Benefits include toning muscles, increases flexibility, stimulating the nervous system and helping to correct postural imbalances.

HIIT Sessions

HIIT (high intensity interval training) sessions are great for anyone looking for a challenging workout. During any HIIT class you will perform a variety of different exercises to work lots of muscle groups, alternating between spurts of intense work and periods of rest.

Spin Classes

Get your heart rate rising and boost your endurance by taking part in our specialist cardio classes. From a great range of cycling classes, to specialist sessions such as Cardio Blast and even Cardio Tennis, there's plenty to choose from to set your pulse racing.

Personal Training

Personal training sessions are a great way to learn new skills and techniques whilst also getting that extra push to go further in your training. Each of our health clubs offers a wide range of qualified personal trainers on-site for our members. If you are just starting out with an exercise programme or you aren't seeing results with your current routine, a personal trainer might be able to help!
