Cardio machines

If it’s a good old cardio-workout you’re after, you’ll find all the equipment you need and more at The Gym Group. Whether you’re training for a marathon, or just want to keep your fitness ticking over, cardio machines are a must for any workout plan. Each machine will get your heart pumping and your pulse racing in different ways – and target different muscle groups too.

Not sure which ones are best for you and your own personal goals? You can talk to one of our friendly instructors. Each of our gyms has such an impressive range of machines, you’ll find it easy to keep your workouts fresh and enjoyable.

Low-impact workout area

This workout area has all the space you need for those low-impact exercises. So feel free to stretch out, warm up, warm down, or even throw a few yoga shapes.

You’ll find all the equipment you need too, from exercise balls to yoga mats and more, helping you to build flexibility, core strength and great posture. All we ask is that you put it back where it belongs after you’ve finished with it, so that the next person who comes along can use it too.
